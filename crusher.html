Products & Key Uses

All materials are produced from black rock using controlled crushing, screening and optional washing for finer grades.

  • Jally (Blue Metal Aggregates) — sizes: 6mm, 12mm, 20mm, 40mm; typical use: concrete mixes, road base and drainage layers.
  • M-Sand (Manufactured Sand) — graded for concrete and masonry; consistent particle shape and controlled fines.
  • P-Sand (Plastering Sand) — fine, washed for smooth plaster finishes and rendering.

Typical technical ranges

  • Fineness modulus M-Sand: ~2.6–3.0 (adjustable)
  • Specific gravity: 2.6–2.8
  • Moisture content (M-Sand): usually <2% (dry on request)
  • Aggregate Crushing Value (ACV): typically <30% for general concrete use

Handling & storage

Store in dry covered area to avoid moisture pickup; use clean vehicles to avoid contamination; sample a small batch before large pours.
